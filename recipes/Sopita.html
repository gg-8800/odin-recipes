Sopita de Conchas

In this recipe, we are going to make a sopa de conchas. This is a fast and easy way to make sopita, but most importantly, a delicious sopa de concha! I am going to walk you through every step of the recipe.

Ingredients

  • shells pasta
  • 4 tomotoes
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • tomato bouillon
  • chicken broth or stock

Steps

  1. Add oil to pot and put on medium heat
  2. Add shells to put and keep mixing until they are a brownish color
  3. Cut tomatoes in half and add them to a blender
  4. Also add onion, garlic and the tomato buillon to blender
  5. Blend the ingredients together
  6. Add blended ingredients to pot w/ the shells and mix until boil
  7. Add chicken broth to pot and mix (add water if you need more liquid)
  8. bring to a boil then turn heat down for 7mins
  9. turn off and let cool down
  10. Enjoy!
