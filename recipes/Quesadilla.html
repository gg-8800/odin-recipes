Cheesy Quesadilla

In this recipe, we are going to make a cheesy quesadilla. This is a fast and easy to make a quesadilla, but most importantly, cheesy and delicious! I am going to walk you through every step of the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Monterey Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Warm up tortillas on the griddle
  2. Cut cheese in slices
  3. Add slices of cheese on to one side of the tortilla
  4. Fold tortilla in half (cheese should be inside)
  5. Keep heating tortilla until cheese is melted
  6. Enjoy!
