Beef Lasagna

Delicious Lasagna

In this recipe, we are going to make a beef lasagna. This is a fast and easy to make lasagna, but most importantly, delicious! I am going to walk you through every step of the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of Beef
  • 12 slices of Lasagna
  • Montery Jack Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olives
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook the meat - 30mins
  2. Boil the lasagna - 10-12mins
  3. Spray down the casserole pan w/ oil
  4. Add 3 pieces of Lasagna to bottom of pan
  5. Then add sauce
  6. Then add meat
  7. Add olives, mushrooms and cheese
  8. Repeat steps 4-7 two more times
  9. Cover pan w/ foil
  10. Bake in over at 375 degrees for 40mins
  11. Add Parmesan cheese on top and enjoy!
